# Social Media Goals & Strategy

## Objective
Build brand awareness, generate leads, and establish thought leadership across Twitter/X, Facebook, and Instagram.

## Platform Strategy

### Twitter/X
- **Frequency:** 3-5 tweets per day
- **Content mix:** 40% thought leadership, 30% engagement, 20% announcements, 10% curated
- **Character limit:** 280
- **Tone:** Professional yet conversational, use threads for depth
- **Hashtags:** 1-3 per tweet
- **Best times:** 9am, 12pm, 5pm EST

### Facebook
- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day
- **Content mix:** 35% case studies, 30% community engagement, 25% announcements, 10% behind-scenes
- **Tone:** Friendly, informative, community-focused
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 per post
- **Best times:** 10am, 1pm EST

### Instagram
- **Frequency:** 1 post per day + 3-5 stories
- **Content mix:** 40% visual storytelling, 30% behind-scenes, 20% motivation, 10% product
- **Tone:** Visual-first, inspirational, authentic
- **Hashtags:** 10-15 per post
- **Best times:** 11am, 2pm, 7pm EST

## Content Pillars
1. **Industry Insights** — Share trends, data, analysis
2. **Success Stories** — Client wins, case studies, testimonials
3. **Behind the Scenes** — Team culture, office life, process
4. **Product/Service Updates** — New features, launches, improvements
5. **Community Engagement** — Polls, questions, user-generated content

## Key Messages
- Innovation drives growth
- Our clients' success is our success
- Technology should simplify, not complicate
- Building the future, one solution at a time

## KPIs (Weekly Targets)
