# Business Goals

## Current Focus Areas
1. **Lead Generation** — Generate 50 qualified sales leads per month via LinkedIn outreach
2. **Thought Leadership** — Publish 3 LinkedIn posts per week on AI automation and productivity
3. **Brand Awareness** — Increase company LinkedIn followers by 25% this quarter
4. **Client Engagement** — Share case studies and success stories from recent projects

## Tone & Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven insights where possible
- Always include a call-to-action (CTA)
- Use relevant hashtags: #AIAutomation #Productivity #SalesLeads #DigitalTransformation

## Post Templates
### Lead Generation Post
> Struggling with [pain point]? We helped [client type] achieve [result] using [method]. DM me to learn how.

### Thought Leadership Post
> [Insight about industry trend]. Here's what this means for [target audience]: [key takeaway]. What are your thoughts?

### Case Study Post
> [Client] was facing [challenge]. After implementing [solution], they saw [metric improvement]. Here's the full story:

## Scheduling Preferences
- Best posting times: Tuesday-Thursday, 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM
- Avoid weekends and holidays
- Space posts at least 24 hours apart
